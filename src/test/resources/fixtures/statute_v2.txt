Statute on Public Information Access
Draft 2 — 2026-02-14

Article 1
The purpose of this statute is to establish the right of citizens to access information held by public bodies.

Article 2
All public bodies shall act in accordance with the principles of transparency, accountability, and good governance.

Article 3
Any decision affecting the rights of citizens must be issued in written form and shall contain explicit reasoning.

Article 4
Appeals against administrative decisions shall be filed within twenty-one days of notification to the affected party.

Article 5
Repeated violations of this statute shall be reported to the supervisory authority within five working days.
