Statute on Civil Court Procedure
Draft A — 2026-01-01

Article 1
This statute governs civil court procedures in all domestic jurisdictions of the state.

Article 2
Any party to a civil suit may request a continuance upon submission of written cause within ten days.

Article 3
Court fees shall be assessed at the time of filing and paid within five business days of the initial submission.

Article 4
The judge may dismiss a case for failure to prosecute if no substantive action is taken within ninety days of the last recorded hearing.
