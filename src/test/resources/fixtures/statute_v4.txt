Statute on Civil Court Procedure
Draft B — 2026-02-01

Article 1
This statute governs civil court procedures in all domestic jurisdictions of the state.

Article 2
Any party to a civil suit may request a continuance upon submission of written cause within fifteen days.

Article 5
The judge may dismiss a case for failure to prosecute if no substantive action is taken within ninety days of the last recorded hearing.

Article 6
All judgments must be published in the official government registry within thirty days of their issuance.
