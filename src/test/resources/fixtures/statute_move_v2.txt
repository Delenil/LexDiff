Statute on Data Protection
Version B

Article 1
All personal data shall be collected and processed only with the explicit consent of the data subject.

Article 5
Any breach of personal data security must be reported to the supervisory authority within seventy-two hours of discovery.

Article 6
Data subjects shall have the right to request erasure of their personal data within thirty days of the request.

Article 7
The controller shall implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to ensure data security and confidentiality of the processed data.
